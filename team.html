Meet the Team

The Turtle Crew is a group of Cartographic and Applications Specialists that are passionate about the environment and wildlife, combining this with their skill in GIS to create effective web solutions.

Naini Singh

A 2002 liner, she's a lover of both science and literature. From managing inventory to sales in pharmacies, her career journey has been diverse. Passionate about storytelling, she's translated comics, written novellas, and edited manuscripts. With a Bachelor's degree in Geology, she's now pursuing a GIS Cartographic Specialist course at Fleming College, Ontario. Outside of work and studies, you'll find her playing violin and piano, enjoying trekking and camping trips, and spending quality time with her pet dog.

Jordan Steinman

Jordan is currently attenting Fleming College, in the Geographic Information Systems - Applications Specialist program. Prior to this, Jordan also acquired 3 additional diplomas - In 2017, Jordan kickstarted her academic journey by attending the Civil Engineering Technician Program at Georgian College and learned skills in AutoCAD, Microsurvey, and project management. Following this, she attended Fleming College's Fish and Wildlife Technician program and took a third year in the Forestry Technician Program to complete her Dual Diploma. Giving her a many new environmental and field skills, these programs aided in allowing her to continue learning in-depth about one of the things she is passionate about - the environment.

Rylee Goerlitz

Rylee is currently enrolled in the Geographic Information Systems: Application Specialist program at Sir Sandford Fleming College - School of Environmental and Natural Resources Sciences and has recently graduated from the Ecosystem Management Technician Program also at Fleming College. Additionally, she is a graduate of Brock University gaining a BSc in Bioloigcal Sciences. All of these programs have highlighted the importance of GIS technology in conservation, providing precise spatial analyses that allow for the planning and management of ecosystems. She has experience working with ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, and AutoCAD. Along with her experience in the environmental field, Rylee has worked with the Kawartha Conservation Authority. There she further developed her skills in ArcGIS Pro, gained experience surveying dams/bridges, and contributed to the collection of LiDAR data for floodplain mapping. All these opportunities have helped Rylee develop hands-on expereicne in both the field and office.

Laura Cobham

Laura is currently enrolled in the Geographic Information Systems: Application Specialist program at Fleming College, but previously attended Wilfrid Laurier University, graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Geography and Geomatics. She has experience collecting GPS points in the field to map elevation changes, and earned the Esri Canada 2023 Scholarship where she expanded on this work. She also has a background in graphic design, and enjoys using GIS technology and graphic design softwares together, especially in the context of ArcGIS StoryMaps.
